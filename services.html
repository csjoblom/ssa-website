Academic Services


Advising Services

In addition to helping students with class scheduling and degree choices, advisors encourage and mentor student-athletes throughout their entire college experience. It is the department's philosophy to foster an honest and open learning environment in which students learn to become more self-reliant.

Tutorial Program

Tutoring is available for all student-athletes. Our tutors are hand picked and trained to work with student-athletes. Appointments are available during evening and day times to accomodate student-athletes' busy schedules.

Study Table Program

Study tables are available during normal operating hours, evenings and Sundays. All students have access to quiet study tables and computer workstations. Students with laptops can connect to the campus network through wireless access point

Under Prepared and At-Risk Services

Our team of learning specialists provide high level support for at-risk students and students with disabilities. Learning specialists also coordinate incoming academic screening as well as psychoeducational evaluations and accommodations with the Accessible Education Center.

Math and Science Lab

The Math and Science Lab are open Monday through Friday, 10:00am to 2:00pm, for drop-in help with math and science courses of all levels.

Information Technology Services

We house serveral student computer labs with full access to productivity software, internet and printing services. In addition, our Grahics Lab provides services for drawing, image editing, film production, and web design. Computer Lab Monitors and IT Consultants are available to support students with problems on their personal computers as well as for any general IT inquiry.
